Stories of hope - The Lives we've changed.


Isabel is a thirteen year old girl who lives with her 3 siblings, her mum and dad. Her dad works construction when he can to support Isabel and the family, although he often finds himself out of work.

Anil is a young boy from South East Sri Lanka , who use Hope for Children's support to better his education.

Radhika is a 15-year-old girl from Nepal. She lives with her parents and elder sister who is currently pursuing her bachelor’s degree in Kathmandu.

Rashmi is a young girl from a remote area of Sri Lanka called Buttala. She is 13 years old and thanks to Hope for Children, she has discovered a passion for education that just a few years ago was not even a possibility.


Our Mission:-

"HOPE® creates life-changing wishes for children with critical illnesses. We seek to bring every eligible child’s wish to life because every child deserves a childhood. Make-A-Wish has become the world’s leading children’s wish-granting organization, serving children in nearly 50 countries worldwide. Together, generous donors, supporters, staff and more than 43,000 volunteers unite to deliver hope to wish kids and their families when they need it most. Please join us by making your donation to help us grant more life-changing wishes come true!"
